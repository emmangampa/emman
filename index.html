Story

Conclusion

Through our project “Plugging into the Future”, we successfully explored and visualized electricity consumption patterns across various regions, timeframes, and sectors using Tableau. The interactive dashboards provided clear insights into peak usage periods, regional demand differences, and the impact of real-world events like lockdowns. These findings not only highlight the importance of data in energy management but also support better planning, policy-making, and sustainability efforts.
